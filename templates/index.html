VISUALIZATION OF THE UNDERGROUND EXCAVATION

A normal day in the mining industry where employees are expected to report to work and use the shaft to go at times 3-5km underground, upset the virgin stress nature of the Earth's crust to extract the mineral of economic interest which could be gold, coal, diamond etc at a planned excavation. At such depth, a slide miss-calculation can cause catastrophic events that would not only lead to loss of machinery and delay in production but also lost of lives that would leave children to become orphans. The Maximum Unsupported Span API will ensure that errors are drastically reduced, mining operations optimized, informed and intelligent decisions be made and above all lives being saved.

ABOUT

The Maximum Unsupported Span API is an application programming interface designed to enhance the safety within the mining Industry. This API makes use of multi-modal machine learning ensemble-algorithms (Classifiers and Regressors) to bring about an accurate prediction of the Maximum Unsupported Span (MUS) that will result in cost reduction, mining optimization and the overal improvement of safety thereby safe-gaurding the human lives.
